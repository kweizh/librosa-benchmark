2. **Self-similarity matrix** — `librosa.segment.recurrence_matrix` with `mode='affinity'`, `metric='cosine'`, `full=True` → (646 × 646) dense cosine-affinity SSM.

3. **Foote checkerboard kernel** — custom 40 × 40 kernel (half-size = 20 frames): +1 in top-left / bottom-right quadrants, −1 in off-diagonal quadrants, tapered by a 2-D Gaussian (σ = 10). No pre-built shortcut used.

4. **Novelty curve** — kernel slid along the SSM diagonal via a Frobenius inner-product at each frame index, then smoothed with a 1-D Gaussian filter (σ = 10 frames) and normalised to [0, 1].
