`/workspace/solution.py` implements the full fingerprinting pipeline:

2. **Spectrogram**: Magnitude STFT is computed (`n_fft=2048`, `hop_length=512`) and converted to dB scale via `librosa.amplitude_to_db`.

6. **Confidence**: Computed as the ratio of matches at the winning offset to total matches.
