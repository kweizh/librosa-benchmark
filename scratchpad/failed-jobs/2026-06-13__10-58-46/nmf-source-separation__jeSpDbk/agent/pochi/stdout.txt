### Approach

1. **STFT**: Computed complex STFT (2048-point FFT, 512 hop) to get magnitude and phase.
2. **NMF Decomposition**: Used `librosa.decompose.decompose` with `sklearn.decomposition.NMF` (nndsvda init, 2000 iterations) to factor the magnitude spectrogram into 4 spectral basis vectors and their time-varying activations.
3. **Soft Masking (Wiener-like)**: Instead of using the NMF-approximated magnitude directly (which loses fidelity), each component's magnitude proportion relative to the total NMF reconstruction is used as a soft mask applied to the **original** complex STFT. This ensures the sum of all component STFTs exactly equals the original STFT.
4. **ISTFT**: Each masked complex STFT is inverted back to a time-domain waveform.
