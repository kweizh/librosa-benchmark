### Pipeline

1. **Load** `/workspace/mixture.wav` (mono, 22050 Hz, 220500 samples) via `librosa.load`.
2. **STFT** with `n_fft=2048`, `hop_length=512`, `win_length=2048`, Hann window, `center=True` → complex spectrogram `D_mix` (1025 × 431).
3. **NMF decomposition** via `librosa.decompose.decompose(S_mix, n_components=4, sort=True)` → `components` (1025 × 4) and `activations` (4 × 431). Each component's spectrogram is `outer(components[:,k], activations[k,:])`.
4. **Wiener soft masks**: `mask_k = S_k² / Σ_j S_j²` (magnitude-squared ratio). Masks sum to 1 everywhere.
5. **Masked iSTFT** per component: `D_k = mask_k * D_mix`, then `librosa.istft(..., length=n_samples)` to guarantee exact sample count.
6. **Spectral statistics** computed from each resynthesized waveform (centroid via `librosa.feature.spectral_centroid`, RMS from signal energy).
